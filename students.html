Why This Campaign Is for You

I understand what it's like when student voices go unheard, or when school feels more stressful than it needs to be. I want to be someone who represents your concerns and ideas with sincerity and care.

My goal is to improve communication, make events smoother, and bring a calm, steady presence to student leadership. I want to help create a school year that feels more efficient, fun, and focused on all of us.
