For Teachers & Staff

Dear Teachers,

Thank you for everything you do behind the scenes to keep this school running. As a student leader, I aim to bring a mindset of organization, responsiveness, and proactive effort to the student council.

My goal is to serve as a bridge between the student body and faculty—to listen, communicate clearly, and support the school’s vision with integrity and consistency.
